## Center for Optimal Living
<p class="bigText">Visual identity for the <a href='https://www.centerforoptimalliving.com'>Center for Optimal Living</a>, an NYC-based treatment and training facility that offers integrative harm reduction therapy for addiction and mental health issues.</p>


I worked up two brand concepts for the Center. The first evolved the Center's existing materials, focusing on what they called the "spark of inspiration" in the mind of a patient on her journey to health. The second, more adventurous concept was inspired by the Center's approach to harm-reduction therapy, an integrative method for creating new perspectives. In this I found a parallel to Cubism, which also integrates multiple perspectives into something new, and created a visual system that could shift, move, and evolve.
