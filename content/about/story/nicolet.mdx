title: "Nicolet High School"
order: 1
---

Public high school exposed me to diversity, providing an invaluable expansion of consciousness in America’s most segregated city. I was fortunate that the arts were well funded at Nicolet; the education must have worked, as I soon won statewide competitions in writing and drawing. I also discovered my analgesia for the most common phobia: public speaking was a delight. I tried debate, but I'd barely learned to twirl my pen when I was drawn to theater, where I landed roles like “The Nerd” in _The Nerd_ and “The Man Who Came to Dinner” in _The Man Who Came to Dinner_. And, of course, we did do <a target='_blank' href='https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnHRVzKoNN0&mode=theatre'>_Oklahoma._</a>
